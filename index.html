What is HackTheGold?

HackTheGold is PYLUSD's first student hackathon. Students from high schools throughout Southern California will converge at El Dorado High School for 24 hours of innovation and learning. There will be incredible prizes, and all meals and snacks are provided.

FAQ

What is a hackathon?

Hacking is not about breaking into your school computers. Hackathons are a place where students come together to work on fun projects using technology. It's a great time to hangout with friends, work on something you care about, and have food paid for. Check out this blog post written by a hackathon veteran.

What will I be doing?

HackTheGold is a 24 hour overnight event where teams with the guidance of mentors learn to turn their ideas into working prototypes. Mentors will help everyone, from absolute beginner to grizzled veteran. It's an incredible learning experience. Attend and you won't regret it.

What if I don't have a team or idea?

Just come to the event and we will find you a team. We'll be holding a team making session before the hacking begins. You can also find teammates on Facebook beforehand. Working alone is fine too. Teams can have at most 4 people.

What if I don't know how to code?

No problem! Designers and creatives are very common at hackathons. Want to learn how to code? We can help you out with that too.

How much does it cost?

Absolutely nothing for the event. We'll be providing snacks and meals, and sponsors will be handing out swag and prizes. Unfortunately though, we will not be providing travel reimbursement.

Who can attend?

All high school students are welcome. Middle school students who can behave independently will also be allowed.

What do I need to bring?

A computer (preferably laptop), student ID, chargers, a sleeping bag if needed, and anything you need to maintain hygene.

Can I work on an existing project?

All work has to be done at the event.

How big can a team be?

No more than four people, please don't be one man team. The real fun and productivity happens when you're on a team.

What can I build?

Just about anything. You could make your first website, your first iPhone app, your first Android app, your first robot. The possibilities are endless. Whatever it is, mentors will get you started.
